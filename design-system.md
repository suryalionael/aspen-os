## Brand System

### Brand Reference

Aspen Training Centre Brand Book is the primary source of truth for all branding decisions.

### Visual Reference

https://aspentrainingcentre.ca/

All UI and visual design decisions should align with the Aspen Training Centre website and official brand identity.

### Brand Colors

Primary Colors:

* Aspen Green: #164F40
* Forest Green: #061D10
* Aspen Gold: #D7B745

Secondary Colors:

* Teal: #4B7477
* Light Blue: #879DA1
* Light Gray: #CBCFD0
* Off White: #F4F4F4

### Typography

Application UI:

* Geist

Marketing Pages:

* Baskerville
* Metropolis

### Visual Style

The product should feel:

* Professional
* Human-centered
* Community-focused
* Creative
* Welcoming
* Modern

### Design Principles

* Match the overall visual style of the Aspen Training Centre website.
* Maintain consistency with the official Aspen brand book.
* Use Aspen Green as the primary action color.
* Use Aspen Gold sparingly for accents and highlights.
* Prioritize whitespace and readability.
* Avoid excessive gradients.
* Avoid overly corporate enterprise styling.
* Avoid visual clutter.

### UI Inspiration

* Aspen Training Centre Website
* Linear
* Notion
* Arc Browser

### UX Philosophy

The application should feel simple, calm, approachable, and trustworthy.

Users should be able to understand any screen within 30 seconds without training.
